THE RULES

LEVELS EXPLANATION

Level 1
  • You will play with 4 colors.
  • Speed of the sequence will increase every 5th, 9th, 13th etc.
  • The colors won't change their position.
  • Not available in MODE 2
Level 2
  • You will play with 4 colors.
  • Speed of the game will NOT increase.
  • After the 5th, 9th, 13th etc. sequence, the colors will change their position.
Level 3
  • You will play with 5 colors.
  • Speed of the sequence will increase every 5th, 9th, 13th etc.
  • The colors won't change their position.
  • Not available in MODE 2
Level 4
  • You will play with 5 colors.
  • Speed of the game will NOT increase.
  • After the 5th, 9th, 13th etc. sequence, the colors will change their position.
Level 5
  • You will play with 5 colors.
  • Speed of the sequence will increase every 5th, 9th, 13th etc.
  • After the 5th, 9th, 13th etc. sequence, the colors will change their position.
  • In MODE 2, speed is not affected.

Mode 1 (1 or more players)

Single Players
  • When the game starts, Simon will give you the first signal. Repeat the signal by pressing the same color.
  • Simon will duplicate the first signal and add one. Repeat these two signals by pressing the same color lenses, in order.
  • Simon will duplicate these first two signals and add one.
  • Continue playing as long as you can repeat each sequence of signals correctly.
  • If you fail to repeat a sequence exactly, or if you take more than 5 seconds (10 seconds from level 4) to repeat a signal, Simon responds with "BUZZZZ" sound. This means you've lost and the sequence of signal ends.
2 or more Players
  • Decide who goes first
  • Play as described above except players take turns repeating Simon's signal

Mode 2 (1 or more players)

Single Players
  • When the game starts, Simon will give only the first signal.
  • Repeat Simon's signal, and immediatly add one. In this mode, don't wait for Simon to repeat signals
  • Repeat the first two signals and add one more.
  • Continue repeating the signals of the previous sequence and increasing that sequence by one. Try to reach the longest sequence.
  • If you make an error or take more than 5 seconds (10 seconds from level 4) to play a signal you will hear the "BUZZZZ" sound. This means the game is over.
2 or more players
  • Follow step 1 above.
  • Player 1 repeats the first signal and adds one
  • Players 2 repeats the first two signals and adds one.
  • Play continues with each player repeating the signals of the previous sequence and adding one more.
  • Any player who make an error or takes more than 5 seconds (10 seconds from level 4) to play a signal is given the "BUZZZ" sound and is out of the game. Press the SIMON button to begin a new sequence with the remaining players.
  • The last player left in the game wins!
